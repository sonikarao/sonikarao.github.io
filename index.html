Bio

Well hello there! My name is Sonika Rao and I am a senior at Liberty High School, in Frisco, Texas. I am heavily involved in the Yearbook production at my school, and this year I am serving as the Editor-in-Chief. Along with this, I am involved in the Mock Trial Competitive team, DECA, Destination Imagination, and the Destination Imagination Junior Board on which I have served as Co-Director and Chairman of the DI-me store for the past three years. Aside from school work, I love to jam out to music, talk about politics, drink coffee, tell bad puns, and pet other people's dogs. But the most important thing about me is that I want to make a positive impact on the world.
